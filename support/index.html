Support - FAQ's

Here you can find the most popular questions asked by our customers, if you can't find the correct question please contact us.

Does Slime Hosting have a control panel I can manage my website with?

Yes, Slime Hosting features an enhanced user interface that is fully integrated with cPanel. Our single-sign on control panel gives you access to domain registrations, SSL certificates, and other hosting account features. Our hosting accounts feature cPanel which is the industry leading control panel and provides a rich online environment from where you can manage your web hosting.

Can I monitor my disk space and bandwidth usage?

Yes. You may monitor disk space and bandwidth usage from within your cPanel control panel.

How much does additional bandwidth cost?

If you need additional bandwidth for your account you will need to upgrade to a larger plan. You can upgrade to a larger plan through your client area. The cost will depend upon the plan you select. You can also log into your control panel at any time to view your current bandwidth usage statistics.

How long does it take for my dedicated server to be activated?

We guarantee that all our standard server configurations will be set up within 1 business day. Factors contributing to longer setup times may include server add-ons, custom configurations, and payment approval time.

Where can i redeem my free 30 day Buycraft trial?

You will recieve your free trial link within the email you recieved with your service information, just click the link and your ready to go.

How can I view my FTP details?

You may view your FTP account details through your control panel and navigating to FTP accounts then use the hostname as your domain name.

How long does it take for my DNS records to update?

From the time you change the DNS on your domain name to point to our name servers it will take approximately 12-24 hours for the change to propagate throughout the Internet and become active. During this time your website may be down until the change has fully propagated through the Internet.

Is there a money back guarantee?

Yes, at Slime Hosting we offer a 5 day money back guarantee which all of our customers can take advantage of at anytime within this period.

Can I get a subdomain for my minecraft server?

Yes, get in touch with support or submit a ticket and ask for your FREE subdomain to be activated, they will get back to you once your chosen subdomain is setup.
